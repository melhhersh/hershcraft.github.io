Why Italy?

So we've gotten this question a lot and believe it warrants a little explanation. While studying abroad in London, we spent a week traveling across Italy, seeing the sights and tasting all the amazing cuisine. We fell in love with the country and wanted to come back (with a little more money). When our friends got married, we found some of the best moments were when we all were hanging out before the ceremony even began. It was this inspiration that led us to this trip. Italy will be a chance for our closest friends and family to come together and celebrate with us. We wont need to panic about spending a small amount of time with everyone, but instead focus on those who matter most to us....whilst in the beautiful Italian country side.


THE WEDDING

When

September 12, 2020

Where

Relais La Cerreta

Umbria, Italy

ACCOMMODATIONS

The bridal party will the be staying at the villas at Relais La Cerreta. As it gets closer to the date we will post some additional accommodations in the area.

We are staying at the Villa Sept 10th - 13th, guests are welcome to book additional nights at the villa directly with the villa if they wish. Events will be on the 11th and 12th, so feel free to arrive whenever you are able on the 10th or the morning of the 11th.

The area is a 2 hour train ride from Rome, with the region roughly in the middle between Florence and Rome.

TRAVELING

We have booked our flights to come in early and stay past the wedding to enjoy Italy. The closest airport that has the best range of flights is Rome. We'd recommend renting cars if you want to be able travel to small towns around the area, but it is possible to take the train to the nearest town and taxi to the villa. We encourage you to set google flight alerts or use hopper to book your flights, we've seen deals between $300-$500.
